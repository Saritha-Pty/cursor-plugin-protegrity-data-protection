# Anonymization

Use this skill when the customer wants to anonymize values in structured data.

## Customer goal

Help the customer remove identifying information while keeping data useful for analysis or testing.

## When to use

- When the customer wants to anonymize records
- When the customer needs safer datasets for sharing
- When the customer wants to reduce identity exposure in structured data

## What the customer needs

- Docker Desktop
- Protegrity AI Developer Edition anonymization service

## What this skill should do

1. Ask the customer for the dataset or schema.
2. Confirm the anonymization goal.
3. Run the anonymization workflow.
4. Summarize what changed in plain language.
5. Keep the customer in control of the final output.

## Guidance

- Preserve usefulness where possible.
- Remove identity exposure without creating MCP or extra setup.
